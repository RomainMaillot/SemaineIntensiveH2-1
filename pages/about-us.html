Who am I ?

My name is Ben. I’m twenty-six years old and I don’t need a governess. I live in Brooklyn, New York, which The New York Times calls “Brooklyn.” I’m a writer and a comedian. I also compose music and illustrations. As a hobby, I count upward. I began at 1 but I’ve made—if I may flatter myself—considerable progress since then.

Why DRYP

I always loved plants, I love them so much I have more than 15 at home ! All of them have different needs, some of them have to be water daily, some once per week, and some almost never. I keep plants in a place they are not supposed to be, a Brooklyn apartment, that’s why I’m building DRYP with the few friends I met. I have a dream that, maybe one day, we will stop killing our plants and finally find out that we can learn much things from plants if we take the time to pamper them.
